My primary goal is to build openly, support academic research, and provide a strong toolkit for students, developers, and non-commercial projects. You are highly encouraged to fork, modify, and self-host this software for those purposes!

### Why AGPL-3.0?
To protect my freelance work and ensure the sustainability of this project, this software uses a copyleft license. Under AGPL-3.0, if you use this software in a commercial product, integrate it into a proprietary system, or offer it as a managed service, **you must release your entire source code as open-source** under the same license.

### Commercial Licensing
If you are a company, agency, or enterprise that wishes to use this software internally or commercially *without* open-sourcing your own proprietary code, you can easily obtain a commercial license. 

This allows you to bypass the AGPL-3.0 restrictions and use the software privately in your business operations.
